Experiment 1:Reaction Time

Description:This experiment measures how quickly individuals can respond to various stimuli.

Objective:To access how reaction times vary under different conditions(e.g., noise levels,time of day).

MethodologyParticipants were tested with visual and auditory stimuli at different times and under various conditions. Their response times were recorded.

Results:Results showed that reaction times were slower in noisy environments compared to quiet ones.Additionally,performance improved with practice.
